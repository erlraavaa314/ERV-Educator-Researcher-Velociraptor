How I view mentoring

I believe that my role as an advisor is to encourage you to explore your own project and ideas on your own terms. You are the main character and you decide for yourself how you want to do things. My job is to guide you whenever you feel stuck and to suggest new directions when you feel lost. To help myself live up to these aspirations, I've made a short list of guiding principles that I try follow.

  • This is your project.
  • I am on your side.
  • I will be patient.
  • I take your project seriously.
  • I encourage you to be independent.
  • Your time is as important as mine.
  • I will not take credit for your work.
  • I want to see you when you make progress.
  • I want to see you when you're stuck.
  • My job is to offer you advice, but you decide if you want to take it.
  • That you are happy is more important than these guidlines.
In short, I want you to feel free and supported.
